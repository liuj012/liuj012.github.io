june 16, 2020

i dreamt that me and a few other poeple from school were playing basketball. then we ended up at my house in taiwan in the garage. we were doing art with ms. misty*. then ms. misty went to the main house** to get something and a storm began. halfway through the storm, ha an*** told me i got my period so we were going to go to the main house to get pads but then it was too foggy and windy so we decided not to. while we were still staring at the house, it caught on fire and i was screaming for people to call an ambulance since ms. misty was still in teh house. no one could reach the hopsital phone. then grandpa arrived and i woke up.

it's interesting to reread this now (on mar 20, 2024) because i still remember exactly what the dream looked like. it's weird.

*ms. misty was my art teacher
**at my grandparents house in taiwan, they had a detached garage.
***ha an is my friend who i've known since 6th grade
june 18, 2020

i had a lemon meringue tart from saint honore

saint honore is a chain bakery in vietnam. i always had it at ciputra. ciputra is a gated community in west lake. a good amount of my friends lived there.

i remember when we would hang out, usually it would be the morning after a sleepover, we would make our way to saint honore and have ourselves a lemon meringue tart.

july 1, 2020

going swimming at night
i just swam alone at night. i felt so peaceful floating on the water and looking up at the night sky. all i could hear was the running water. makes me want to write a poem about how i feel, too bad i'm terrible at wiritng poems

the summer of 2020 was one of the nicest and most relaxing summers i've ever had. i think maybe part of it is because of the lack of tourism because of covid. vietnam was so empty and nice. at this point, i had left ho chi minh city and reconvened with my parents at cam ranh, a beach town near nha trang in the middle of vietnam. we were staying at a resort that just opened, and we had a villa with a pool.
july 4, 2020

arriving at intercon in danang

the intercontinental hotel in danang is the most beautiful hotel i have ever stayed in. i still think about it a lot. because of covid, there was a really good discount for the hotel and my dad had booked for us to stay there for a few nights, and they were the best nights of my life. everything about that place is incredible!!! you're in this secluded mountain area in danang, and there's a private beach, where across you can see the city of danang. and the sky is soo incredibly clear and the weather is beautiful and the place is a paradise.
july 6, 2020

driving a jet ski by myself

this is still at intercon. my dad's friend has a friend who lives in danang and owns jet skis. we were invited by them to go to this restaurant that was floating in the ocean, very secluded area, and he had his jet skis there for us to use. my dad's friend took me on the jet ski and we sped into the middle of the ocean and he gave me the opportunity to operate it on my own. it was a really nice day. we were just out on the open water. i think jet skis are a lot less scary when you're the one operating it.

august 10, 2020

had khoai mi after school

khoai me is essentially steamed cassava in coconut milk that is topped with a salty sesame mix. it's a treat from the southern part of vietnam and my aunt would always bulk buy it for me everytime i visited my grandparents in saigon (the south). i remember this time, my aunt had sent a big package of it to me in hanoi.
august 16, 2020

having a picnic at ciputra

my friend hoai le and i had planned to have a picnic at this park in ciputra, where she lived, and i remember it started pouring mid-picnic and we had to pack up and wait for her parents to come pick us up. we ended up just eating the food we had prepared in her parent's bedroom by the window while it continued to rain.
